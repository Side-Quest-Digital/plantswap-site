🌿 PlantSwap Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

PlantSwap is built by Side Quest Digital. We only collect what we need to make the app work, store it in the EU, and never sell it. This policy explains exactly what we collect and why.

Who we are

PlantSwap is operated by Side Quest Digital. If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at privacy@sidequestdigital.co.uk.

What we collect and why

Account information

Your name and email address, provided when you sign in with Google or Apple. Used to identify your account and sync your data across devices.

Food scan images

Photos you take or select from your gallery are sent to our AI service (OpenAI GPT-4o via OpenRouter) to identify ingredients and generate a vegan recipe. Images are not stored by PlantSwap unless you choose to save the recipe. OpenAI processes images per their own privacy policy.

Scan results

The ingredients, recipe, and nutritional data generated from each scan. Stored in our database for signed-in users to enable quota tracking and recipe history. Guest scans are not stored.

Saved recipes

Recipes you explicitly save, including the food photo and generated recipe data. Stored in your account until you delete them.

Preferences

Your dietary requirements and favourite chefs. Stored in your profile so we can personalise recipe suggestions.

Usage analytics

App events such as "scan completed" and "recipe saved" are sent to PostHog (EU servers) to help us understand how the app is used and fix problems. Events are linked to your user ID but contain no message content or image data.

Subscription status

Whether you are on the Free or Pro plan. Managed via RevenueCat and stored in your profile. Purchase transactions are handled by Apple or Google — we do not store payment card details.

Guest scan count

If you use PlantSwap without signing in, we store a scan counter on your device only (using local storage). This is never sent to our servers.

Third-party services

We use the following services to operate PlantSwap:

  • Supabase (EU) — database, authentication, and file storage. Privacy policy
  • OpenAI via OpenRouter — AI analysis of food photos. OpenAI privacy policy
  • PostHog (EU) — usage analytics. Privacy policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Privacy policy
  • Google Sign-In — authentication. Privacy policy
  • Apple Sign In — authentication. Privacy policy

Data storage and security

All user data is stored in the European Union. We use Supabase with row-level security, meaning your data is only accessible to you. Connections are encrypted in transit using TLS.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the UK or European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and all associated data.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@sidequestdigital.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

To delete your account and all data, go to Settings → Sign out and then email us. We will delete your account within 7 days.

Data retention

  • Account and profile data: retained until you request deletion.
  • Scan results: retained until you delete them or your account.
  • Saved recipes: retained until you delete them or your account.
  • Analytics events: retained for 12 months per PostHog's default policy.

Children

PlantSwap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of PlantSwap after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
